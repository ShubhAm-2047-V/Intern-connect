RED FLAGS TO WATCH OUT FOR

IF YOU SEE THESE SIGNS ON OTHER PLATFORMS, PROCEED WITH CAUTION.

  • ASKING FOR MONEY: Legitimate internships never ask you to pay for training, equipment, or "security deposits".
  • VAGUE JOB DESCRIPTIONS: Be wary of listings that don't specify responsibilities or skills required.
  • UNPROFESSIONAL COMMUNICATION: Use of personal emails (gmail.com, yahoo.com) instead of company domains.
  • INSTANT OFFERS: Getting hired without an interview or proper assessment is highly suspicious.
